why selka?

March 27, 2025 fitness

selka started as a CREST project, but it quickly became something much bigger. our goal was simple: create an app that designs personalized workouts using AI. but we didn’t stop there. we refined it, expanded its capabilities, and made it better every day to ensure that users get the most effective and engaging fitness experience possible.

unlike generic workout apps, selka doesn’t just give you a preset routine. instead, it tailors each workout based on your unique profile, including:

  • body weight and height – so exercises scale to your physique.
  • available equipment – no gym? no problem. selka works with what you have.
  • experience level – from beginner to pro, workouts evolve with you.
  • personal goals – whether you want strength, endurance, or flexibility, selka adjusts.
  • daily updates – workouts are refreshed every day for maximum variety and progress.

what makes selka different?

  • personalized adjustments: the ML adapts your workouts based on your progress, ensuring they stay challenging but achievable.
  • continuous improvement: by analyzing your performance and feedback, selka fine-tunes your routines over time.
  • real-time feedback: it provides instant adjustments to ensure you’re always moving in the right direction.
  • preventing burnout: the AI keeps the intensity balanced, preventing both plateaus and overtraining.
